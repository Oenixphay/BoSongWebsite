What to See

Click for more info!

  • Rittenhouse Square
    A beautiful public park surrounded by historic homes and luxury buildings. It's a great spot for a picnic or to relax while watching the local scene.

  • The Barnes Foundation
    A world-class museum housing an impressive collection of Impressionist, Post-Impressionist, and early modern paintings, as well as African art.

  • Philadelphia Museum of Art
    Though technically located near Fairmount, it's just a short walk from Rittenhouse Square and home to iconic art collections, including the famous "Rocky Steps."

What to Do

Click for more info!

  • Explore Rittenhouse Square
    The square itself is the neighborhood's centerpiece, attracting residents and visitors alike. You can often find local artists, musicians, and events happening throughout the year.

  • Shopping on Walnut Street
    Rittenhouse is known for its upscale shopping, with boutiques and high-end stores lining Walnut Street.

  • Dine at a High-End Restaurant
    Rittenhouse has a range of fine dining options, making it a great place to treat yourself to a special meal.
