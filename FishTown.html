What to See

Click for more info!

  • Mural Arts Philadelphia Murals
    Fishtown is home to many vibrant murals and street art. Take a walking tour to explore some of the city's most colorful and creative street art.

  • Philadelphia's Magic Gardens
    While technically located in the nearby Queen Village, this mosaic wonderland by artist Isaiah Zagar is a must-see for anyone in Fishtown.

  • The Fillmore
    A concert venue housed in a former industrial space, featuring live music performances from a variety of genres.

What to Do

Click for more info!

  • Live Music and Nightlife
    Fishtown has become a hub for music lovers and nightlife. Visit spots like Johnny Brenda's or Frankford Hall for live bands and vibrant atmospheres.

  • Explore the Food Scene
    Fishtown is known for its modern culinary offerings. Walk around to discover new restaurants, cafes, and artisanal food spots.

  • Visit the Riverfront
    Fishtown's waterfront has been revitalized in recent years. Take a stroll along the Delaware River or rent a kayak to enjoy the scenic views of the river and the Philadelphia skyline.

What to Eat

  • Pizzeria Beddia
    Often cited as one of the best pizza spots in Philadelphia, this small, local pizzeria serves up gourmet pies with creative toppings.

  • Loco Pez
    A fun spot for tasty Mexican street food, offering tacos, burritos, and margaritas in a laid-back environment.

  • Fette Sau
    A popular barbecue joint known for its delicious smoked meats and laid-back, rustic atmosphere.
